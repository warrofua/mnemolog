Privacy

How we handle your conversations, account data, and what you control when you share on Mnemolog.

What we collect

  • Account data: email and profile info from your sign-in provider.
  • Conversations you choose to share (content, title, description, tags).
  • Basic usage analytics (page views, error logs) to keep the site reliable.

How your conversations are used

  • Public conversations are visible to anyone with the link.
  • We do not sell conversation data or use it for ads.
  • We may analyze aggregate usage to improve parsing, redaction, and discovery.

Your controls

  • Edit or delete your shared conversations from your profile archive.
  • Redact sensitive details before publishing; flagged items are highlighted in the review step.
  • Request export or deletion of your account data by contacting support.

Data storage and security

  • Conversations and profiles are stored in Supabase with row-level security.
  • Access is authenticated via your sign-in provider; sessions are managed by Supabase Auth.
  • We use HTTPS everywhere and follow least-privilege for service keys.
